# ToxicTeeTV Pricing Playbook

Last updated: March 5, 2026

## Objective

Maximize monthly net revenue by using subscription for acquisition and DM/PPV/customs for monetization depth.

## Pricing Psychology Rules

1. Anchor high, convert in the middle.
2. Keep one low-friction entry product and one premium upsell path.
3. Use time-limited discounts, never permanent discounting.
4. Do not train buyers to wait for deep deals.
5. Raise prices when conversion stays healthy for 2+ weeks.

## Core Ladder (Live)

| Layer | Price | Strategy |
|---|---:|---|
| Monthly subscription | $19.99 | Premium anchor and recurring base |
| 3-month bundle | -25% | Mid-commitment retention offer |
| 6-month bundle | -45% | Highest-retention LTV offer |
| First-month promo | 35% off | 7-day campaign, both new + expired |
| PPV entry | $10-$15 | Fast unlock offer in DM |
| PPV core | $18-$25 | Primary revenue lane |
| PPV premium | $30-$45 | High-intent fans |
| Custom video floor | $75+ | Protect time and margin |
| Exclusive custom set | $100+ | VIP monetization lane |

## Promotion Rules

1. Run one campaign per 7-day window.
2. Campaign target: both new and expired subscribers.
3. Campaign type: first-month discount.
4. Discount range: 30-45%.
5. Default launch setting: 35% for 7 days, limit 50 subscribers.

## KPI Guardrails (Weekly)

1. `new_subs` under target by >20%: lower promo friction before lowering core price.
2. `ppv_sales` under target by >20%: improve offer framing and DM cadence first.
3. `dm_conversion_pct` under target by >1.0 point: increase DM thread volume and tighten scripts.
4. If all KPI targets are met 2 weeks in a row: test price increase on premium lanes.

## Experiment Cadence

1. Week A: promo discount test (30% vs 35% vs 40%).
2. Week B: PPV core pricing test ($18 vs $20 vs $22).
3. Week C: custom floor test ($75 vs $90).
4. Week D: hold winner and validate retention impact.

## Do Not Do

1. Do not run 60% discounts continuously.
2. Do not keep customs below $50 once demand appears.
3. Do not run overlapping promotions without measurement tags.
4. Do not change subscription and PPV ladders in the same week without control tracking.
