# TikTok-Safe Content System

## Goal

Drive traffic from TikTok to the link hub without violating platform rules.

## Approved Pillars

1. Archery and skill clips
2. Outfit and style transitions
3. Day-in-life creator routines
4. Confidence and personality-based trends
5. Soft teaser storytelling with no explicit framing

## Hard Rules

1. Keep TikTok output fully SFW.
2. No explicit sexual language in captions, overlays, or comments.
3. No references to meetups or in-person transactions.
4. Avoid language implying illegal activity.
5. Keep CTA generic: "link in bio", "full menu", "exclusive content".

## CTA Bank

1. "More in bio 💕"
2. "Exclusive drops linked in bio"
3. "Daily updates and behind-the-scenes in my bio links"
4. "If you want the full vibe, start with my bio hub"

## Hook Bank

1. "POV: your redhead archer just got competitive 🎯"
2. "Soft vibes, sharp aim, and zero missed shots"
3. "Pick my next outfit in the comments"
4. "Come rate this look from 1-10"
5. "Day in my life, creator edition"

## 7-Day TikTok Cadence

1. Mon: Archery clip
2. Tue: Outfit transition
3. Wed: Lifestyle mini vlog
4. Thu: Trend remix
5. Fri: BTS setup clip
6. Sat: Q&A response video
7. Sun: Weekly recap + CTA
