# Compliance Boundaries

## Non-Negotiables

1. No illegal requests.
2. No in-person meetup arrangements through creator platforms.
3. No unsafe content or coercive requests.
4. No content involving non-consenting participants.
5. No underage themes, roleplay, or implications.

## Platform Safety Checklist (Pre-Publish)

1. Caption is platform-safe for destination channel.
2. No banned language for TikTok/IG.
3. Asset has required participant consent documentation.
4. Offer and CTA match platform policy.
5. Any custom requests stay inside declared boundaries.

## Data Handling

1. Do not commit passwords to git.
2. Store credentials in password manager + local `.env`.
3. Rotate credentials if they were shared in plain text.
4. Separate business finance stack from personal accounts.

## Response Guardrails

1. Price haggles: hold boundaries and remain concise.
2. Pushy or disrespectful messages: disengage quickly.
3. Policy-violating requests: refuse once, then block/report.
