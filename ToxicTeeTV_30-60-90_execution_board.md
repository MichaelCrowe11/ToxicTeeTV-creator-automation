## Risk Register

| Risk | Trigger | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Creator onboarding rejection | Verification failure | Pre-submit ID/selfie QA checklist | Ops/Compliance Lead |
| Content deactivation | Policy breach | Publish gate + weekly moderation QA | Ops/Compliance Lead |
| Payout delays | Processor/bank delays | Escalation SOP + documentation pack | Ops/Compliance Lead |
| Tax non-compliance | Missing forms/records | Filing calendar + monthly reconciliation | Finance/Tax Advisor |
| High churn | Weak retention cycle | Segmented DM flows + winback offers | Growth Analyst |
| Revenue volatility | Over-reliance on one offer | Diversify subs/PPV/tips/promos | Creator Lead |

## Definition of Done (DoD)

1. Task outcome is delivered and documented.
2. KPI impact is measured or explicitly marked as not measurable.
3. Compliance check is passed for any content/account workflow change.
4. Playbook/SOP is updated if process changed.
